# Kairux - Quick Reference Guide

## 🎯 Brand Essentials

**Name:** Kairux  
**Tagline:** Business in Flow  
**Message:** Where business connects and flows seamlessly  
**Domain:** kairux.com (available)

## 📖 Name Meaning

- **Kai** (Greek: καί) = "and" / "connection"
- **Rux** (from "flux") = "flow"
- **Kairux** = "Connecting Flow"

## 🎨 Brand Voice

### Use These Phrases:
✅ "Business in Flow"  
✅ "Seamlessly connected"
